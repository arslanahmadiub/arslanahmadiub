### 🚀 About Me

I'm a Senior Fullstack Engineer with **6+ years** of experience architecting and shipping production SaaS platforms — from real-time auction systems to healthcare ERPs to AI-powered tools. I specialize in the **PERN/MERN stack**, **TypeScript**, and **AWS infrastructure**, and I'm currently building products at **Draftly**, my own SaaS company. Verified **Toptal Top 3%** talent, with client work spanning healthcare, retail, automotive, and media across the US, UAE, and UK.

- 🔭 Currently building **Smart HMS**, **Smart Inventory**, and **Smart LMS** at Draftly
- 🧠 Expanding into **AI engineering** — recently added Python/FastAPI alongside my core TypeScript/Node.js stack
- 🛠️ Daily tools: **Claude Code**, **Cursor**
- 🌍 Based in Lahore, Pakistan (UTC+5)
